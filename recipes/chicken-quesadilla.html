Chicken Quesadilla

Description

A quesadilla is a Mexican dish consisting of a tortilla that is filled primarily with cheese, and sometimes meats, spices, and other fillings, and then cooked on a griddle or stove

Ingredients

  • Flour tortilla
  • Shredded cheese
  • Small chicken pieces
  • Oil
  • Yellow onion
  • Taco Seasoning
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Add chicken and taco seasoning to pan. Cook for 4 minutes
  2. Add onion for 5 minutes. Set aside
  3. On clean skillet add butter, then tortilla. Add cheese on one half of tortilla and fold.
  4. Cook until tortilla is golden
