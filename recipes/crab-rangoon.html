Crab Rangoon

Description

a dish consisting of deep-fried wontons filled with a mixture of crab meat and cream cheese.

Ingredients

  • 18 wonton wrappers
  • Imitation crab meat
  • Cream cheese
  • Sesame oil

Steps

  1. Place 1 tsp of filling in each wonton
  2. Fold one of the corners up to form a triangle
  3. Fry or bake
