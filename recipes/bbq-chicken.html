BBQ Chicken

Description

Barbecue chicken consists of chicken parts or entire chickens that are barbecued, grilled or smoked.

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken breasts
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • BBQ sauce

Steps

  1. Drizzle olive oil, salt, and pepper onto chicken breasts
  2. Grill 4-5 minutes on each side
  3. Add BBQ sauce, and cook for 2 minutes on each side
